A NEW CLOTHING BRAND by NOBLE DENIM THAT DELIVERS QUALITY CLOTHES AT A FAIR PRICE.

In our ongoing passion and desire to bring manufacturing jobs back to the United States we are excited to introduce a new brand. Victor focuses on men’s and women’s basics made of organic and sustainable materials in our co-op factories in the U.S.A. We believe the people who make our clothes are hugely important, so we are giving 5% of our profits back the the factories we partner with.

Great clothes should not break the bank. With accessible prices and easy, free home try on boxes you will be able to get great clothes at a fair price.
